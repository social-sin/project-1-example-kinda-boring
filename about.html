About Me

hi! I'm Kieran, the author and artist of the comic. I'm from Nevada, so please enjoy this drawing of an abandoned schoolhouse outside of one of the many ghost towns here. These two characters were the very first things I drew when I started drawing five years ago and I've been trying to figure out how to tell a story with them ever since. This is me shoving them in the big narrative blender and pressing Puree.
